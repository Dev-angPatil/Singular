# Singular

Enterprise treasury infrastructure for the Company of One. An AI-driven autonomous financial co-pilot for the freelance and creator economy.

Traditional banking was built for the plural—for corporations with accounting departments, payroll teams, and enterprise CFOs. It treats the solo creator, the independent consultant, and the freelance engineer as an anomaly.

Singular flips the paradigm. It is an autonomous cash-flow and treasury engine engineered specifically for the individual. In physics, a singularity is a point of infinite density and power; in the modern economy, a solo creator is a singular force capable of driving a multi-million dollar business alone. Singular acts as their automated, AI-driven backend infrastructure—abstracting away the volatility of solo business ownership so they can focus entirely on their craft.
